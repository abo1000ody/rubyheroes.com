Top Level Domain (TLD) library : Remarks

The list of TLDs is not static but subject to change. More or less recent changes involved the removal of FX for France (European Territories) and the addition of PS for Palestinian Territory, Occupied.

A good source of up to date information about top level domains is the International E-mail Accessibility site by Olivier M.J. Crepin-Leblond.

Note that his site does not have information about the TLDs listed in RFC 2606 (aka BCP 32) that are valid but only for very special purposes.
